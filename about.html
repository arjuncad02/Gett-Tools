Started in a garage.
Built for the trades.

GETT Tools is an independent, small business based in North Vancouver, British Columbia — still run by the same small team that designed our first product.

Our Story

How we got here

2019

A prototype in a garage

Frustrated with dragging heavy stacked PACKOUT cases across job sites, our founder machined the first PAC-EEzE prototype by hand.

2021

First 100 customers

Word spread through local trade crews. Every early kit was built, packed, and shipped from a single home workshop.

2022

Etsy storefront launches

GETT Tools opens an online storefront, making PAC-EEzE and replacement wheel kits available across Canada and the US.

2024

1,000 customers milestone

GETT moves into a proper warehouse in North Vancouver and brings on its first additional team members.

2025

Manufacturing expansion

New tooling and a larger assembly floor let GETT cut lead times and begin work on the PAC-EEzE V2.

2026

1,400+ customers & PAC-EEzE V2

Today, GETT ships nationwide with a growing dealer network — while every kit still passes through the same small team.

Mission

Make the job site move faster.

We build mobility and organization systems that remove friction from the workday — so tradespeople spend less time hauling gear and more time doing the job.

Vision

Every trade, fully equipped.

We want every PACKOUT system on every job site in North America riding on a GETT mobility kit — designed and built by people who actually use the tools.

Manufacturing Process

From design to your door

The same five-step process runs on every single order, no matter the size.

01

Design

Every part is modeled and toleranced to fit the PACKOUT™ platform exactly.

02

Machine

Steel components are precision cut and formed to spec.

03

Assemble

Hand-assembled and torque-checked by our warehouse team.

04

Inspect

Every kit is test-fit before it's boxed for shipping.

05

Ship

Packed and shipped from North Vancouver, typically within 48 hours.
