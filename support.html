Frequently Asked Questions

Orders & Compatibility

Will GETT products fit my Milwaukee PACKOUT?

Yes. Every GETT mobility and organization system is designed and test-fit specifically to the Milwaukee PACKOUT™ modular platform.

How long does shipping take?

Most orders ship within 48 hours from our North Vancouver warehouse. Delivery is typically 2–5 business days within Canada and 4–8 business days to the US.

Can I order in bulk for a fleet or crew?

Yes — contact our support team directly for fleet and bulk-order pricing on any product line.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently we ship across Canada and the United States. Reach out if you're outside these regions and we'll see what we can do.

Warranty & Returns

What's your warranty policy?

All GETT hardware is covered by our standard 1–2 year workmanship warranty (see individual product specs). Register your product using the Download Center's warranty form to activate coverage.

How do I return or exchange a product?

Reach out through our Contact page within 30 days of delivery. Our small team handles every return personally — no call centers.

What if a part arrives damaged?

Contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll ship a replacement part at no cost once verified.

Installation

How difficult is installation?

Most GETT kits install in 10–20 minutes with basic hand tools. Every kit ships with a printed install guide, and PDF/video guides are available in the Download Center.

Do I need special tools to install PAC-EEzE?

No — PAC-EEzE installs with a standard hex key, which is included in the box.
